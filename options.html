Options Ticker Format

Options tickers follow the format: O:UNDERLYING+YYMMDD+TYPE+STRIKE

Example 1: O:SPY251219C00650000

SPY Call expiring Dec 19, 2025 at $650 strike

Example 2: O:AAPL241220P00150000

AAPL Put expiring Dec 20, 2024 at $150 strike

Example 3: O:TSLA250117C00300000

TSLA Call expiring Jan 17, 2025 at $300 strike

Format Breakdown:

  • O: - Options prefix
  • SPY - Underlying ticker
  • 251219 - Expiration date (YYMMDD)
  • C - Call (or P for Put)
  • 00650000 - Strike price ($650.00)
